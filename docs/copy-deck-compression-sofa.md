# EzHome — Compression Sofa: Full Copy Deck

### Conversion Copywriting · Ogilvy Framework
##### Brand Voice: Professional / Casual / Bold
##### Target: Metro renters & first-home buyers, 24–45, mid-to-upper income
##### Goal: Conversion (purchase / add-to-cart / pre-order)

---

> **Copywriter's Brief**
>
> The compression sofa solves one of the most universal pain points in urban living: getting a premium sofa through tight stairwells, narrow doors, tiny lifts, and past strata rules — without sacrificing comfort or aesthetics. The buyer is smart, style-conscious, and has either already suffered through a moving nightmare or is about to. They want "premium" but won't overpay. They move often. They change layouts. They need a sofa that respects the way they actually live.
>
> **Emotional triggers deployed:** Relief (no more moving anxiety), pride (design they're proud of), urgency (limited drops / waitlist), authority (engineering + materials), social proof (community of movers who get it).

---

## Figma Layer Naming Convention

Each copy block below includes:
- **Layer name** — matches your Figma component layer
- **Tag** — HTML semantic (H1, H2, H3, body, caption, CTA)
- **Char count** — exact character count for layout sizing
- **Copy** — the final text
